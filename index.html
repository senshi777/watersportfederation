Message from the President

A Warm Welcome to Our Global Community

On behalf of the Board of Directors and the entire team at the Water Sport Federation, it is my honour and privilege to welcome you to our growing global community.


Water sports have long been a bridge between nations, cultures, and generations. Whether you are a trainer shaping the swimmers of tomorrow, a club seeking international recognition, or an athlete working toward your next certification — you now belong to a federation built on the values of safety, integrity, and excellence.


Our mission is clear: to establish and uphold the highest international standards across all water sports disciplines, and to ensure that every certificate issued under our name represents real, verified competence. Through our QR-verified certification system, we are bringing transparency and trust to water sports credentials worldwide.


We are proud to be a Swiss-based non-profit, committed to the public good — not private interest. This is your federation. Welcome aboard.

Mr. Mohammad Almassri
President · Water Sport Federation
Our Disciplines

Five Sports. One Federation.

The WSF governs and certifies practitioners across five world-class water sports disciplines, each with structured pathways from beginner to advanced.

🏊
Swimming
Foundational aquatic competency — from first strokes to advanced technique. Includes dedicated infant swimming programs.
🤽
Water Polo
International rules, referee certification, and coaching standards for competitive water polo.
🏄
SUP
Stand Up Paddleboarding instruction and safety certification — flatwater, coastal, and touring disciplines.
🚣
Kayaking
Paddle sports coaching pathways covering flatwater, sea kayak, and whitewater environments.
🛟
Lifeguarding
Pool and open-water lifeguarding certification aligned with international rescue and safety protocols.
Certification Pathways

Internationally Recognized Credentials

All certifications are valid for one year and must be renewed annually. Each certificate carries a unique QR code for instant verification.

Level 1
🌊
Beginner
Swimming · Level 1

Foundational water confidence, floating, basic strokes (front crawl, backstroke), and elementary water safety awareness. Suitable for first-time swimmers of all ages.

Renewal: Every 12 months
Level 2
🌊🌊
Intermediate
Swimming · Level 2

Developed stroke technique (breaststroke, butterfly), turns, endurance, and introduction to open-water awareness. Demonstrates consistent, independent swimming ability.

Renewal: Every 12 months
Level 3
🏆
Advanced
Swimming · Level 3

All four competitive strokes, race starts, flip turns, sustained endurance, and open-water proficiency. Prepares swimmers for competitive and leadership pathways.

Renewal: Every 12 months
Infant I
🐠
Aqua Baby
Infant Swimming · Level 1

Designed for infants aged 6–18 months. Focus on water familiarization, breath control, parent-assisted floating, and creating positive early aquatic experiences in a safe environment.

Renewal: Every 12 months
Infant II
🦈
Aqua Toddler
Infant Swimming · Level 2

For toddlers aged 18 months–3 years. Introduces independent movement, submerging, jumping into the pool with support, and foundational self-rescue awareness.

Renewal: Every 12 months
Infant III
⭐
Aqua Junior
Infant Swimming · Level 3

For children aged 3–6 years. Builds on independent swimming, basic stroke patterns, underwater swimming, and essential water safety for young learners.

Renewal: Every 12 months

Certificate Verification

Scan. Verify. Trust.

Every certificate issued by the Water Sport Federation carries a unique, tamper-proof QR code. Employers, clubs, and institutions can scan any certificate in seconds to confirm its authenticity — no account or login required.

  • 1

    Scan the QR Code

    Using any smartphone camera or QR reader, scan the code printed on the physical or digital certificate.

  • 2

    Instant Secure Redirect

    You are directed to a secure verification page on watersportfederation.org — no third-party apps needed.

  • 3

    View Certificate Details

    The page displays the holder's full name, certification level, issuing trainer, issue date, and expiry date.

  • 4

    Validity Confirmed

    A clear Valid / Expired / Not Found status is shown instantly. No certificate can be forged or altered.
